Thai Fried Rice

Description

In Thailand, fried rice is a very typical street food. It is available at every restaurant that serves stir fried dishes.

In this recipe you will learn how to make a delicioius plate of fried rice just like you'd find in Thailand.

Let's get this cooking started!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups of cooked Jasmine rice
  • 5-10 shrimp, head peeled, but tail on
  • 1/2 small white onion
  • 1 leaf of Chinese broccoli
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • pinch of sugar
  • 1 tbsp oil

Steps

  1. Peel and mince garlic
  2. Slice 1/4 of the small white onion into medium strips
  3. Dice 3-4 green onions
  4. Slice Chinese broccoli in half - first along the spine - then slice into 1 cm sized strips
  5. Turn stove on to medium-high heat and heat up your wok or frying pan. Then add 1 tbsp of oil
  6. Once oil is hot, add the garlic and stir fry continuosly for approximately 15 seconds or until nice and fragrant
  7. Add your shrimp and fry until they start to turn pink and become firm
  8. Add about 1/2 of the rice first. This will soak up all the shrimp juice and oil. Stir fry for about 10 more seconds. This is a little rice trick.
  9. Move all your rice to one side of the pan, and then crack in the egg into the empty side
  10. Whisk the egg, let it cook for a few seconds, and then add in the rest of your rice, and give it a quick stir
  11. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, a pinch of sugar and continue stir frying your rice, making surw all the sauce gets mixed in
  12. Add your Chinese broccoli and onions, stir fry for another 30 seconds until the vegetables wilt a bit
  13. Lastly toss in your green onions, stir for a few more seconds and then you're done
  14. Slice a few wedges of lime and serve it on a plate next to the rice
